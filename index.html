Frequently Asked Questions

What is PetCare App?

PetCare is a smart pet care assistant that helps you track your pet’s health, mood, grooming routines, and weight. It also provides AI-powered health assessments based on your pet’s data.

How does the AI health assessment work?

Our AI analyzes your pet’s weight trends, mood history, and care routines to provide insights into their overall well-being. It gives you recommendations based on breed, age, and activity level.

Can I track multiple pets?

Yes! You can create individual profiles for each of your pets and track their care routines separately.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use secure cloud storage and follow strict data protection policies to ensure your and your pet’s information is safe.

Can I access my pet’s data on multiple devices?

Yes, your pet’s data is securely stored in the cloud, so you can access it from any device using your account.
